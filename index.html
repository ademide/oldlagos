Rethinking the City.

Since its founding, the city of Lagos has seen waves of reinvention along its shores. One recent period of innovation in the city produced new architecture, infrastructure and cultural institutions. These activities, centered around the Racecourse, Marina and Broad St gave rise to buildings, people, places and notable events which together we now think of as “old Lagos.”
